About me

Technology

I’ve been studying technology since 2011, and I’m specialized in computer networks, servers, wireless and web/mobile programming.

I did an scholarship program at the University of Toronto, Canadá, and took some courses in the Computer Science major, besides an academic writing course, focusing on scientific articles production.

I’m an undergraduate student at the Federal University of Ceará in Brazil under the major of Technology in Computer Networks.
Most of the knowledge I acquired through those years was self-taught, researching on the internet, practicing tutorials, chatting with friends, and hands on trying, failing and trying again.

Writing

I started writing two years ago on a notebook and also on a personal blog, called mundodeotavio.ml, which has texts in english and portuguese with purpose of sharing my ideas, thought and observations about my favorite topics, allowing myself to have full text-creation freedom.

I’m an assiduous reader and I’m always trying to keep myself actualized on the innovations on subjects I love and have great importance to me, such as good life, career, self-development, entrepreneurship, music, philosophy, culinary and biographies.
I like to watch great movies, TV shows, YouTube channels and documentaries on the most diverse topics to enrich my repertoire of knowledge and opinion and really appreciate the filmmaking art.

Another old passion of mine is the music. I’ve been floating around the most diverse genres and types of music since I was a kid, and I love finding something new, that really catches me and draws my attention and curiosity. Because of that love for music, I taught myself how to play guitar; Thenceforth, sometimes I attempt to compose songs and arrange them, but just as an amateur — They don’t usually come as nice as I expected :(

I practice meditation, yoga, Muay Thai and bike around a lot.
People usually say that I have a tremendous ease in learning and absorbing new things, and I feel pretty confident to talk or write on any topic.

Experience

I started working at the age of 16 as an office assistant at the Northeast Bank of Brazil (Banco do Nordeste do Brasil) in Fortaleza, CE.
From them on I’ve developed a enormous ability and ease with text editing and spreadsheet tools, being able to handle huge chunks of data, formulas and macros.

I’ve also worked as an assistant for Itau-Fiat bank, in a car dealership called Icavel, where I was dealing directly with the customer service, helping them to choose the best offers and taxes for their financing.

Currently I’ve been working as autonomous IT technician for an outsourcing company, helping government agencies and participating in a startup project called Autoboot, which aims to deliver services and consulting for business in our geographical bounds. We want to offer a complete assistance on technology management to these business, helping them building and evolving their websites, apps, visual identity, marketing strategies, social networks, technological innovations and IT management.
